# Go-To-Market & Launch Strategy

The roadmap to acquire the first 100 paying customers within 6 months of Vave HRM product launch.

## 1. Beta Testing Launch (Months 1-2)
- **Target Audience:** Warm network, local agencies, and startups (10-50 employees).
- **Offer:** Free access for the first 3 months in exchange for detailed feedback and video reviews.
- **Goal:** Reach 10 active tenants, gather UX feedback, test payroll calculations.

## 2. Public Launch (Month 3)
- **Launch Channels:** Product Hunt, Hackernews, Twitter (X), LinkedIn.
- **Pricing:** Introductory discount ($1/employee/month) for early adopters.
- **Content Marketing:** Publish blueprints comparing "Manual Payroll Processing vs Automated Vave HRM".

## 3. Growth Acceleration (Months 4-6)
- **Organic SEO:** Content hubs around compliance, leaves, and payroll processing templates.
- **Affiliate Program:** Incentivize HR consultants to recommend Vave HRM to their clients.
- **Paid Acquisition:** Targeted search ads for keywords such as "Affordable HR Software for SMEs".
