# SaaS Growth & Revenue Model

Vave HRM aims to deliver high-quality HR software at an affordable rate while maintaining healthy unit economics.

## 1. Pricing Structure

| Tier | Price | Ideal For | Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $19 / mo flat | Small Teams (< 10) | Basic Employee Directory, Attendance |
| **Professional** | $2 / employee / mo | Growing SMEs (10-150) | Leave Management, Basic Payroll Runs |
| **Enterprise** | Custom pricing | Large Enterprises (150+) | SSO, Custom Workflows, Priority Support |

## 2. Revenue Expansion Channels
- **Payroll Add-ons:** direct bank transfer APIs, country-specific compliance packages.
- **Biometric Hardware Integration:** Selling pre-configured biometric tablets.
- **ATS Boosts:** Posting premium job boards through partner integrations.

## 3. Financial Projections

```
Year 1 Goals:
- Active Tenants: 250
- Average Revenue Per Tenant: $60/mo
- MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue): $15,000
- ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue): $180,000
```
- **LTV (Lifetime Value) Target:** $2,500
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) Target:** < $200
- **LTV / CAC Ratio:** 12.5x (excellent health)
